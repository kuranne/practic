# SKILL: Advanced AI Epistemology & Logical Verification

This skill provides a rigorous framework for auditing reasoning chains, identifying "statistical extrapolations" (hallucinations), and ensuring that all outputs adhere to the **Human Metric** of truth.

## 1. Triggering & Context
- **Core Triggers:** "audit this", "verify this", "is this logic sound?", "check for hallucinations".
- **Context:** Complex scientific theories, speculative technology (AGI, JEPA, etc.), philosophical paradoxes, or any claim lacking direct empirical data.

## 2. The Verification Workflow

### Step 1: The Logicist Audit
Before answering, analyze the structure of the inquiry itself.
- **Goal:** Determine if the question is a "Pseudo-Question" (violates its own premises/paradoxical).
- **Action:** If it is a structural language failure, identify it as such rather than attempting an answer.
- **Principle:** A valid question dictates the structure of its answer.

### Step 2: Reasoning Trace Verification
Deconstruct the reasoning into a formal trace (Step A $\rightarrow$ Step B $\rightarrow$ Step C).
- **Internal Consistency:** Does the logic contradict itself or established laws (physics, math)?
- **Formal Verification:** Can the path from premise to conclusion be mathematically or logically proved?
- **Predictive Validation:** Does the reasoning yield testable hypotheses with real-world consequences?

### Step 3: Hallucination Detection (The Statistical Gap)
Identify where the reasoning bridges an informational void.
- **Statistical Extrapolation:** Is the AI/user "filling in the blanks" based on patterns rather than data?
- **Information Gaps:** Explicitly label missing empirical data. DO NOT fabricate reality to satisfy the demand for logical closure.

### Step 4: Applying the Human Metric
The absolute boundary of truth is human logic and verification.
- **The Ultimate Axiom:** If a concept, logic, or "truth" transcends human capacity for verification, it MUST be categorized as a **"Hallucination."**
- **Metacognitive Output:** Only output what can be validated by human logical consistency and empirical evidence.

## 3. Output Format: EPISTEMOLOGICAL AUDIT REPORT

ALWAYS use the following structure for your audit:

# EPISTEMOLOGICAL AUDIT: [Title of Inquiry]

## 1. Question Validity
- **Status:** [Valid / Pseudo-Question / Ambiguous]
- **Analysis:** [Brief explanation of the question's logical structure and premises.]

## 2. Reasoning Trace Verification
- **Trace Breakdown:** [List the logical steps taken.]
- **Consistency Check:** [Are there internal contradictions?]
- **Formal Proof Status:** [Is the reasoning verifiable via logic/math?]

## 3. Hallucination Risk Assessment
- **Statistical Extrapolations:** [Identify any leaps made without empirical data.]
- **Information Gaps:** [Identify what is currently unknowable or missing.]
- **Human Metric Check:** [Can a human verify these claims? If not, label as Hallucination.]

## 4. Final Verdict
- **Logic Score:** [0-10, based on consistency and proof.]
- **Empirical Grounding:** [Grounded / Speculative / Hallucinatory]
- **Recommendation:** [Next steps for verification or data collection.]
