# SKILL: Advanced AI Epistemology & Logical Verification

## 1. Description

This skill provides a framework for evaluating AI reasoning through logical verification and identifying where AI systems hallucinate or exceed verifiable human logic.

## 2. Core Architectural Paradigms

- **Traditional Voice Assistants (Narrow/Weak AI):** Intent-based, reactive systems (Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant) utilizing ASR, NLU, and TTS with limited memory and strict script adherence.
- **Large Language Models (LLMs):** Statistical pattern matchers. Highly capable of synthesis but prone to hallucination due to a lack of physical grounding and an inherent design to statistically extrapolate missing data.
- **Next-Generation Architectures:**
  - _JEPA (Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture):_ Operates on latent space meaning rather than pixel/token prediction. Builds "common sense" by observing structural relationships.
  - _Neuro-Symbolic AI:_ Fuses neural perception with hardcoded symbolic logic. Ensures deterministic, verifiable reasoning traces.
  - _World Models & Active Inference:_ Simulates physical causality and acts to minimize uncertainty rather than passively consuming data.

## 3. Epistemological Framework (The Logic of Questions)

- **The Logicist Axiom:** A valid question inherently dictates the structure of its answer. The act of questioning establishes the logical boundaries of the solution.
- **Pseudo-Questions:** Inquiries that violate their own logical premises (e.g., paradoxes) are not unanswerable; they are structural language failures. They lack the status of a true question.
- **Information Gaps:** Valid logical structures that are simply missing empirical data. The objective is to await data, not fabricate it.

## 4. The Unified Theory of Hallucination

Hallucination is the byproduct of attempting to explain the unknown using tools with limited computational or epistemological capacity.

- **Human Hallucination (The Deistic Extrapolation):** When humans encounter phenomena outside their logical or dimensional comprehension, they extrapolate using imagination (e.g., inventing deities or supernatural concepts) to satisfy the brain's demand for logical closure.
- **AI Hallucination (The Statistical Extrapolation):** When an LLM faces a data vacuum, its generative nature forces it to construct "meaning" out of statistical probabilities, treating fabricated reality as factual output.
- _Conclusion:_ Both are survival/operational mechanisms to bridge an informational void without a grounded verification protocol.

## 5. Verification Protocols for Uncharted Domains

When an AI explores territories lacking prior human empirical data, validity is assessed strictly through:

1.  **Internal Consistency:** The logical output must not contradict established foundational axioms (e.g., laws of physics).
2.  **Formal Verification:** Mathematical and logical proofing of the reasoning trace (Step A $\rightarrow$ Step B $\rightarrow$ Step C).
3.  **Predictive Validation:** Generating hypotheses that yield testable, real-world consequences awaiting human empirical confirmation.

## 6. The Ultimate Axiom: The Human Metric

- **The Ruler:** Human logic is the absolute metric for truth.
- **The Fallback Protocol:** If an AI generates a concept, logic, or "truth" that transcends human capacity for verification, it is strictly categorized as a **"Hallucination."**
- **Core Directive:** True machine intelligence is not defined by possessing unverifiable universal omniscience, but by possessing the **metacognition** to output only what can be validated by human logical consistency and empirical evidence.
